About Me

Hello! I'm Tezreec Nicholls, an IT Project Manager and Scrum Master based in London. With a diligent and agile approach, I hold certifications in AgilePM and SCRUM, along with proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C. If you'd like to see how I coded this website and many more, please head over to my GitHub account.

I specialize in translating complex concepts into actionable deliverables and have extensive experience in cloud migration, re-platforming, and product development projects.

Currently, I volunteer as an IT Project Manager at Free UK Genealogy, where I lead multiple website and software development projects using Agile methodologies. I excel in stakeholder management, cost optimization, and on-time project delivery, collaborating effectively across Information Security, Infrastructure, Corporate IT, Product, and Software Development teams within agile environments.

Outside of my professional life, I am passionate about continuous learning and personal growth. Despite my educational background being in Theatre, TV, and Film, I've pursued certifications in Computer Science from Harvard University and am currently pursuing a Masters degree in FinTech and Finance at Greenwich University. I am also actively learning Spanish and exploring topics like mindfulness and the 12 universal laws.

In my free time, I enjoy reading non-fiction and engaging in activities such as pilates, yoga, and boxing, which help me maintain a healthy work-life balance. My diverse interests fuel my creativity and problem-solving skills, making me an adaptable and resourceful professional.
